10 REASONS WHY IT’S MORE FUN IN THE
PHILIPPINES

1. Whale Shark

See and snorkel near whale sharks in Donsol in Sorsogon

2. PRISTINE AND UNSPOILED BEACHES

Take a dip in the crystal-clear waters of one of many Philippine beaches

3. HIKING

Appreciate nature by hiking along the trails of Mount Makiling

4. KAWASAN FALLS

Go cliff diving, canyoneering or swimming at Kawasan Falls

5. WHITEWATER RAFTING

Go white-water rafting in the Cagayan de Oro River with its electrifying rapids

6. THE FESTIVITIES

Experience Dinagyang Festival – full of parades and street dances in onour of Santo Niño

7. EXPLORE BY THE CAR

Drive the scenic coastal highways of Leyte and Samar

8. TUBBATAHA REEFS

Discover one of the world's best dive sites in Palawan

9. ISLAND HOPPINGS

The Philippines has 7,641 islands waiting to be explored!

10. RICE TERRACES

Walk through the rice terraces in the provinces of Ifugao, Kalinga and other provinces
